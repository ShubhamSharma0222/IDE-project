About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for digital, cyber and technology risk roles.


Through collaboration with universities and industry partners, IDR combines academic insight with practical experience to support organisations operating in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals focused on cyber security and digital risk management.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing AI governance models, risk frameworks and future technology risk research.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement security and risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Who We Serve

Our community includes students, early-career professionals and industry practitioners from sectors such as financial services, technology and critical infrastructure seeking to build expertise in digital and cyber risk.
